Dynamic Sales Tracking & Reporting Dashboard

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard integrated with Excel datasets to track sales performance, identify regional trends and analyze product profitability for a retail client.

Power BI Excel KPI Tracking Data Visualization

Project Goal and Methodology

The primary goal was to replace static monthly reports with a dynamic, filterable dashboard that allowed stakeholders to quickly drill down into sales performance metrics. This included tracking revenue, profit margins and sales volume by salesperson, region and product category.

My methodology involved: Data Cleaning and Transformation using Excel's Power Query and functions like VLOOKUP; Data Modeling in Power BI to establish relationships between sales, products and personnel tables; and finally, Visualization using appropriate charts and slicers to deliver intuitive insights.

By automating the data refresh process and creating calculated measures (KPIs) in Power BI, the management team was able to shift focus from data compilation to strategic decision-making, optimizing resource allocation based on real-time performance data.

Analysis and Development Process

1

Data Acquisition & Cleaning

Gathered raw CSV and legacy Excel data. Used Power Query to handle data type inconsistencies, missing values and structure the data for optimal analysis.

2

Modeling and KPI Definitions

Created relationships between tables (sales fact, product dimension, time dimension). Defined key performance indicators (KPIs) like Gross Margin, Sales Target Variance and Year-over-Year Growth using DAX.

3

Dashboard Design & Testing

Developed wireframes and prototypes focused on user experience. Iteratively built visualizations in Power BI, ensuring fast loading times and responsiveness across devices.

4

Deployment & Training

Published the dashboard to the Power BI Service, configured scheduled data refreshes and conducted training sessions for the sales and executive teams.

Key Technologies Utilized

The solution relied heavily on the Microsoft Power Platform ecosystem for seamless integration, transformation and interactive data consumption across the organization, with a focus on Power BI and Advanced Excel functions.
